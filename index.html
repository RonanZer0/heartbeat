heartbeat

Open-source 2D game engine in C++.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Made with SDL and OpenGL.

Heartbeat is made with SDL (mostly for input) and OpenGL for graphics. Easy-to-use texture and animation classes have been provided for you to use, with useful features from flipping and stretching to easily fine-tuning animations to be just right.

Well-documented, tested code.

Don't spend the whole day trying to figure out what something is or does, just check the respective file's comments or the documentation and find out exactly what it is, what it does, and how to use it properly.

Fast & lightweight.

Heartbeat is fast. No fancy bloat, it's just what you need to create 2D games in C++ without having to do all the tedious, lower-level groundwork, debugging, and perfecting yourself.
